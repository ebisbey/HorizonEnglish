about us

Horizon English offers on-site ESL (English as a Second Language) classes to employees. Our teachers have years of experience teaching language and are bilingual in English and Spanish. We tailor our classes to your needs, whether basic English or industry-specific language.

R

Staff Retention

Offering English classes is an extremely effective method of retaining employees. Few companies offer in-house language training, and employees know this when considering other job opportunities.

C

Convenience

We provide lesson plans tailored to your needs. We travel to your company and teach onsite. Materials are provided by us. All we need is a space to teach in (a conference room or staff room works just fine).

S

Skills

Employees improve their communication skills, leading to better performance as well as advancement in their career. Investing in staff's language abilities benefits employees, customers, and the future of your business.

courses


Basic English

This course teaches necessary language for day-to-day communication. Students will learn how to greet others, ask questions, use numbers, and form sentences with correct pronouns and verbs.


Hospitality


The hospitality course focuses on restaurant and hotel language. Students will feel empowered to communicate with guests, patrons, and coworkers who speak different native languages.


Cleaning Services


This course focuses on language cleaning staff need to communicate with people in the facilities in which they work. Students will learn essential cleaning/maintanence verbs, how to discuss schedules, and vocabulary for supplies.


Construction


Construction crews and contract work teams will benefit from this course, which focuses on communicating with clients and coworkers regarding materials, schedules, building processes and safety.
